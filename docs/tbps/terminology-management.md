# Terminology Management

## Purpose

Keep project terms stable, explicit, and consistent across specs, architecture, and implementation work.

## Required reading

- `docs/TERMINOLOGY.md`
- `docs/SPECS.md`
- Any affected milestone, spec, or architecture document

## Steps

1. Determine whether a term is new, overloaded, or ambiguous.
2. Add or update the term in `docs/TERMINOLOGY.md` when it is durable.
3. Replace conflicting wording in affected documents.
4. Confirm the updated term does not redefine existing concepts accidentally.
5. Cross-reference the term in related specs when clarity helps.

## Validation

- Durable project terms are defined in one place.
- Updated documents use the same terms consistently.
- Definitions remain concise and implementation-relevant.

## Exit criteria

- Terminology is explicit and consistent across affected docs.
- New durable terms are recorded in `docs/TERMINOLOGY.md`.
